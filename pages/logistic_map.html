Logistic Map

The logistic map, x_{n+1} = r x_n (1 - x_n), is a canonical example from Strogatz’s Nonlinear Dynamics and Chaos showing period-doubling and the onset of chaos. We’ll add an interactive cobweb diagram and a bifurcation diagram to explore how behavior changes with the parameter r.
