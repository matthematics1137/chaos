Chaos Simulations

Explore nonlinear dynamics through interactive visuals and animations.

This collection is inspired by Steven Strogatz's classic text Nonlinear Dynamics and Chaos — a tour through fixed points, bifurcations, limit cycles, and strange attractors. We’ll build and extend a set of approachable simulations that reveal how simple rules can generate wonderfully complex behaviors.
